Perfect To Me

You're perfect to me, I don't mean in compatibility. You're perfect, like someone I'd wanna be, or perfect, like stars above the moonlit sea. So beautiful, it's hard to look away, especially the overwhelming fragrance of your perfume spray. Honestly it makes my life less dull and gray, Oh, for you, I'd give my heart on a tray You're amazing, there's no doubt, even if I'm with others, you're all I think about. And sometimes, on the most-busiest days, I wish you were there, because your presence takes all my frustrations away. I used to think you were too cool for me, especially at the start, you always talked so rudely. But when I finally gained your trust, you brushed away my loneliness simply like dust. You have a lot of humour in you, always made me laugh in the little bits A second ago I was angry, now I'm confused, because now I'm suddenly laughing, your effect on me really is true. You're way too hilarious, it's surprising, no wonder it's so fun talking with you. And your smile is just so mesmerizing, You've got a hold of my heart. Why? I have no clue.
